About Us

Helping Hands is a non-profit organization founded in 2010. We work across India to provide education, healthcare, and community support for underprivileged families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities by providing resources, education, and opportunities. We believe in equality, compassion, and service to humanity.
